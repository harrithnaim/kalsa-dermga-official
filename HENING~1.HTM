About

A brand house built around the table

Heningan Group is where Kalsa Dermaga builds and grows its own consumer brands. Our first is Heningan Pizza, and the pipeline behind it is a deliberate one — we would rather launch a small number of things properly than a long list of things adequately. Each brand has to earn its place: made well, priced honestly, and worth recommending to a friend.
