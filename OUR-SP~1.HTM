Kalsa Dermaga Presents

Our Sports Club

A social club that happens to play sport. We use the game as the excuse — the reason people actually turn up is everyone else who does. Open to our team, their families, and anyone in the community who would rather meet over a match than over a meeting.

Join a Session
Programme

What a week looks like

Weekly

Futsal Nights

Mixed teams drawn on the night, so you play with different people each week. Ability genuinely does not matter — turning up does.

Weekly

Badminton Socials

Doubles rotation, everyone gets a court, nobody sits out for long. The easiest session to bring a first-timer to.

Weekend

Run & Walk Group

A steady loop at a pace that lets you hold a conversation. Walkers are not an afterthought here; they are half the group.

Always

Makan After

The part that matters. We block out time after every session because most of what the club is for happens once the game has finished.

Monthly

Members' Mixer

An open evening for members to introduce what they do and what they are looking for. Sport gets people in the room; this is where the room gets used.

Seasonal

Family Open Days

Sessions built so members can bring partners, parents and children along — smaller games, longer breaks, more food.
