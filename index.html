About Us

A new digital partner in maritime logistics

Incorporated in March 2026, Kalsa Dermaga Sdn Bhd is a shipping agency and single bill landbridge operator built entirely on web-based platforms — no legacy paperwork, no physical terminal overhead.

From our offices in Kuantan and Shah Alam, our team coordinates shipping agency operations and landbridge documentation for clients across Malaysia, with a growing commitment to the coastal communities we serve.

— Web-based shipping agency operations
— Single bill landbridge documentation
— Offices in Kuantan & Shah Alam
— Community & environmental programmes
